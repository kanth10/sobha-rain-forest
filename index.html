Sobha Rain Forest

Sobha Aspirational Homes - Rain Forest a new budgeted apartment by Sobha Developers is open for booking.


Sobha Rain Forest will consists of 6000 apartment units spread over 81 acres of land. One and two bed room apartment size will range from 600 to 1200 Square feet. This project by Sobha is mainly concentrated on competitive price unlike other projects. Looking into the wide spread development, price will be main selling point as Bengaluru real estate being unaffordable over past few years.

Excellent Location Sobha Rain Forest:

When it comes to real estate investment location is the major factor one would look into. “Sobha Rain Forest” is a best investment and living option aptly located in mid of golden Quadrilateral project junction in Panathur Road, Bengaluru. Four Major roads connecting this project are Marathahalli Road, Outer Ring Road, Varthur Road and Sarjapur Road. It is also closely located to best hospitals, schools, colleges, malls and other recreation centres.

Towards east we have Whitefield which is the Bengaluru’s first IT corridor. Electronic City lies towards west of this project. Closest to the project is Marathahalli and Outer ring road which are busiest IT landmarks. Towards North we can find Hebbal and International airport.

About Sobha Developers:

Founded in 1995, Sobha Limited, a `24 billion company, is one of the fastest growing and foremost backward integrated real estate players in the country. It means that the company has all the key competencies and in-house resources to deliver a project from its conceptualization to completion.

Sobha is primarily focused on residential and contractual projects. The Company’s residential projects include presidential apartments, villas, row houses, super luxury & luxury apartments and plotted developments. In all its residential projects, the company lays strong emphasis on environmental management, water harvesting and highest safety standards. On the contractual projects side, theCompany has constructed a variety of structures for corporates including offices, convention centres, software development blocks, multiplex theatres, hostel facilities, guest houses, food courts, restaurants, research centres, and club houses.
